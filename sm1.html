Statistical Methods I

Course Overview

Statistical Methods is a subject that demands patience, dedication, and careful study. Our professor, Dr. Debasis Sengupta, guided us through various interesting aspects of statistics, though some topics were left uncovered. Here, I have compiled our notes, assignments, and solutions, many of which I worked on myself with contributions from my friend Sagnik Roy. While your professors may cover different material, this collection is worth a read.
